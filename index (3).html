Must-See Spots

Don't miss these specific highlights when you visit Mumbai

Gateway of India

The Taj Mahal Palace

Turn around to see this majestic 1903 heritage hotel, offering the best background for photos.

Statue of Shivaji Maharaj

A beautiful bronze statue located just a few steps from the iconic arch.

Marine Drive

Art Deco Buildings

Look across the road at the UNESCO-recognized row of 1930s Art Deco apartment blocks.

Nariman Point Skyline

Walk to the southern tip to view Mumbai's business district glowing at night.

CSM Terminus

The Star Chamber

Step inside to see the incredible vaulted ceiling and stained glass of the ticket hall.

BMC Headquarters

Stunning municipal building—especially beautiful when lit up after dark.

Elephanta Caves

The Trimurti Sculpture

The main attraction: a massive, 20-foot tall three-headed carving of Lord Shiva.

Cannon Hill

Take a short trek past the caves to see old Portuguese cannons and a great view of the sea.

Juhu Beach

Prithvi Theatre

A legendary theatre just a short walk away. Even if there's no play, the open-air cafe is iconic.

ISKCON Temple

A massive, peaceful marble temple complex located right near the shoreline.

Shree Siddhivinayak

The Golden Spire

Look up before entering to see the Kalash (spire) plated with over 35 kilos of pure gold.

Phool Gali (Flower Alley)

Walk through the vibrant, sweet-smelling alley of vendors selling marigolds and sweets for offerings.
